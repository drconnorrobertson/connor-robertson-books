About Dr. Connor Robertson

Dr. Connor Robertson is an entrepreneur, author, and strategic advisor whose work sits at the intersection of acquisitions, tax strategy, and business systems. His books have helped thousands of readers rethink how they build wealth, structure deals, and run their organizations.

Through Elixir Consulting Group, Connor advises business owners on growth, acquisition strategy, and operational excellence. He is the host of The Prospecting Show and founder of The Pittsburgh Wire and The Grant Finder.
