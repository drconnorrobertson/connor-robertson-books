The 7 Minute Phone Call

Restart Stalled Conversations and Move Deals Forward

The 7 Minute Phone Call is Dr. Connor Robertson's field-tested framework for the single most underrated skill in business: picking up the phone. In an era of email chains, CRM automations, and social media outreach, the direct phone call remains the fastest way to restart a stalled deal, reconnect with a prospect, or move a relationship forward. This book gives you the exact structure, scripts, and mindset to make every call count, all in seven minutes or less.
