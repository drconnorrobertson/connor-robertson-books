Chapter 9: The Referral Conversation

Home / The 7 Minute Phone Call / Chapter 9

A warm introduction multiplies your effectiveness. Chapter 9 explores how to leverage your network, ask for introductions strategically, and create referral conversations that open doors others can't reach.

The Power of Warm Handoffs

A cold call has a 1-2% connection rate. A warm introduction has a 30-40% connection rate. When someone you respect introduces you, the prospect's entire posture shifts. They're curious rather than guarded, interested rather than dismissive.

Asking for Introductions

Most people won't volunteer introductions. They need to be asked clearly and specifically. "Who do you know at TechCorp who we should talk to?" is infinitely more effective than "Can you help me expand my network?" Make asking easy by providing context.

Building a Referral Culture

Great salespeople don't just ask for introductions -- they create conditions where introductions flow naturally. They stay in touch with referral sources, provide updates on outcomes, and celebrate successful connections.

Key Takeaways

  • Warm introductions increase connection rates dramatically
  • Ask clearly and specifically for introductions
  • Make introductions reciprocal -- introduce referral sources to valuable contacts
  • Follow up with referral sources immediately and specifically
  • LinkedIn reveals connection paths for warmer outreach
"Every person has a network. The best prospectors don't build networks -- they activate the networks already around them through warm introductions."
