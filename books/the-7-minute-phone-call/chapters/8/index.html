Chapter 8: Scaling Your Outreach

Home / The 7 Minute Phone Call / Chapter 8

Making 10 calls per week is difficult. Making 50 calls per week is a system. Chapter 8 reveals the frameworks that scale prospecting without burning out.

The Daily Dialing System

Volume prospecting requires structure. Time-block prospecting into 2-3 hour windows dedicated to calling. No emails, no meetings, no interruptions. You enter a flow state where back-to-back dialing becomes a rhythm. Energy management matters -- take breaks every 15-20 calls.

Call Tracking and Metrics That Matter

You can't manage what you don't measure. Track calls made, conversations started, commitments scheduled, and conversion rate. These metrics become your performance dashboard. If you're making 50 calls but only speaking to 8 people, your research or voicemail approach needs improvement.

Pipeline Discipline

When you're making 50+ calls weekly, you need a system for tracking who you spoke with and when follow-up is due. A simple CRM becomes essential. Follow-up is where most prospectors fail.

Key Takeaways

  • Time-blocking dedicated to calling creates flow state
  • Track conversations-per-call and conversion rates
  • Script with flexibility -- practice until delivery feels natural
  • CRM discipline ensures commitments aren't forgotten
  • Reframe rejection as information, not failure
"Scaling from 10 calls to 50 isn't about working harder -- it's about working smarter through systems, tracking, and energy management."
