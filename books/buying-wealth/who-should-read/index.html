Buying Wealth was written for people who are ready to take action. While the concepts apply broadly, these are the specific audiences who will get the most value from the book.

First-time investors

First-time investors looking to understand how to acquire their first real asset and build a foundation for long-term wealth.

W-2 professionals

W-2 professionals earning good incomes but not building equity, who want to shift from trading time for money to owning assets that work for them.

Small business owners

Small business owners interested in diversifying beyond their primary business into real estate or other acquisitions.

Real estate investors

Real estate investors who want a more systematic approach to evaluating and acquiring properties.

Anyone tired of conventional financial advice

Anyone tired of conventional financial advice who recognizes that saving and budgeting alone will not build generational wealth.

Not Sure If This Book Is for You?

If you are asking yourself whether you need this book, consider this: the people who benefit most from Buying Wealth are those who recognize that their current approach is not getting them where they want to go. If you are ready for a new framework, a new system, or a new way of thinking about buying wealth, this book was written for you.
