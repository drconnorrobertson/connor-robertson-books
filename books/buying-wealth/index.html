Buying Wealth

A Practical Guide to Building Ownership

Buying Wealth challenges the conventional wisdom that wealth comes from high income. Instead, Dr. Connor Robertson lays out a systematic framework for acquiring assets that generate returns, using leverage as a strategic tool rather than a liability, and building the disciplines required for long-term financial growth. Drawing on years of experience in acquisitions and tax strategy, this book provides a roadmap for anyone ready to shift from earning wealth to owning it.
