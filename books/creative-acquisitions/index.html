Creative Acquisitions

Unconventional Strategies for Buying Businesses

Creative Acquisitions is the playbook for entrepreneurs who want to buy businesses but do not have deep pockets or private equity backing. Dr. Connor Robertson draws on real-world deal experience to show how creative structuring, seller financing, earn-outs, and partnership arrangements can make acquisitions accessible to a much wider range of buyers. This book is about thinking differently about deals and finding paths to ownership that conventional wisdom overlooks.
