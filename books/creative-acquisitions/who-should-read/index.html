Creative Acquisitions was written for people who are ready to take action. While the concepts apply broadly, these are the specific audiences who will get the most value from the book.

Aspiring business buyers

Aspiring business buyers who want to acquire a business but lack the capital for a traditional purchase.

Entrepreneurs

Entrepreneurs looking to grow through acquisition rather than organic growth alone.

Search fund operators

Search fund operators seeking creative deal structures beyond the standard leveraged buyout model.

Business brokers and advisors

Business brokers and advisors who want to expand their toolkit for getting deals closed.

Anyone interested in entrepreneurship through acquisition

Anyone interested in entrepreneurship through acquisition as a path to business ownership without starting from scratch.

Not Sure If This Book Is for You?

If you are asking yourself whether you need this book, consider this: the people who benefit most from Creative Acquisitions are those who recognize that their current approach is not getting them where they want to go. If you are ready for a new framework, a new system, or a new way of thinking about creative acquisitions, this book was written for you.
