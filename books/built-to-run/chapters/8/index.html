Chapter 8: Technology Stack for Automation

Home / Built to Run / Chapter 8

Your time is your scarcest resource. Chapter 8 reveals how to deploy technology and automation to systematically remove yourself from business operations. The paradox is this: when you engineer yourself out of the business, the business becomes more valuable and you become more free.

The Technology Stack That Scales

Every business needs foundational tools: accounting systems that categorize transactions automatically, CRMs that centralize customer relationships, project management platforms, and communication tools. The key is integration -- when tools talk to each other, they eliminate manual data entry.

Automating the Repetitive

Document every process your business performs, then identify which steps are repetitive and rule-based. These are automation candidates. Email sequences, invoice reminders, expense categorization, data entry, and report generation -- these are often 80% of your overhead.

AI Assistants in Your Workflow

Modern AI assistants can handle customer inquiries, draft responses, summarize documents, and generate reports. They don't replace judgment, but they eliminate rote cognitive work. Define clear guidelines for AI-assisted tasks and they handle volume without your involvement.

Key Takeaways

  • Integration beats proliferation -- a few interconnected systems eliminate more work
  • Automate repetitive, rule-based processes first
  • AI assistants eliminate rote cognitive work without replacing judgment
  • The endgame is systems operating without you
  • Technology executes strategy; it doesn't create it
"Technology doesn't make you less necessary -- it makes you unnecessary for routine work so you can focus on what only you can decide."
