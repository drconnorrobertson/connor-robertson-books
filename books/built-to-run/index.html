Built to Run

Building Business Systems That Work Without You

Built to Run is the book for every founder who has built something successful but cannot step away from it. Dr. Connor Robertson shares the frameworks, systems, and mindset shifts required to transform an owner-dependent business into one that runs on its own. From documenting processes to hiring the right people to building a culture of accountability, this book provides a practical roadmap for the hardest transition in entrepreneurship: going from operator to owner.
